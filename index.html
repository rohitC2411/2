Kensuke's Kingdom

Kensuke's Kingdom is a fiction novel written by Micheal Morpurgo. It is about a boy that has gone sailing with his family who believe they can go around the entire world. But during their quest the boy falls into the water desperate to get his football that his friend gave him. Unfortunately, he gets stranded on a desert island all alone, but thats not what is true.
